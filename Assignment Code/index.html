What is S.M.A.R.T.?

SMART is an acronym that is used as a criteria for goal setting and completing objectives. While there are many alternatives and different interpretations of what S.M.A.R.T. stands for, in GOALR, we take S.M.A.R.T. to stand for

  • Specific -
    target a specific area for improvement
  • Measurable -
    define a measurable indicator for progress
  • Assignable -
    assign the goal to someone
  • Realistic -
    make the goal realistic
  • Time-bound -
    set dates to achieve results
